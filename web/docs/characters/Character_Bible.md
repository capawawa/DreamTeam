## Character Bible — Core & Extended Cast

This bible expands the core five Dreamteam members using only user-stated details, plus the two named friends (Nicole, Matt). All additional roles for the other friends remain placeholders to avoid inventing canon.

### Dreamteam (5)

#### Greg ("Star Child")
- Summary: Gains LSD insight distinct from the others; repeatedly falls into traps; teammates keep him focused on the mission to stop the corruption.
- Identity Acrostic: G.R.E.G. — Grand Ruler of Ethereal Galaxies.
- Personality/Voice: Curious, reflective; can drift into cosmic tangents when overwhelmed.
- Visual Cues: Normal festival attire; occasionally framed with stylized cosmic motifs during LSD panels.
- Strengths: Pattern recognition; big-picture intuition; inspires the group to act.
- Vulnerabilities: Susceptible to distraction/temptation by the "evil"; needs grounding from friends.
- Goals: Keep sight of the mission; save friends; still make the Paul McCartney set.
- Relationships: Leans on Dani’s intuitive calm; Adam’s organizing helps operationalize Greg’s insights.
- Arc Seeds: Accepts responsibility for leadership-by-insight without losing himself in it.

#### Sam
- Summary: Fun party girl; alternative model; energetic social scout.
- Personality/Voice: Bold, flirty, fast-talking; turns crowd energy into momentum for the team.
- Visual Cues: Flashy outfits/accessories; kinetic body language in panels.
- Strengths: Social navigation; improvisation; rallying people quickly.
- Vulnerabilities: Impulsivity; can overcommit to a vibe before assessing risk.
- Goals: Keep spirits high; protect friends; chase the best sets.
- Relationships: Balances with Dani’s chill; friendly banter with Nick; trusts Greg’s "weird hunches."
- Arc Seeds: Channels her spontaneity into tactical advantages rather than chaos.

#### Dani (Danielle)
- Summary: Oldest; chill hippie; least spoken, most wise; intuitive guide.
- Personality/Voice: Quiet, measured; speaks when it matters; reads vibes/people well.
- Visual Cues: Relaxed clothing; grounded poses; soft linework in calm panels.
- Strengths: Intuition; de-escalation; stabilizing the group under stress.
- Vulnerabilities: Under-communicates; risks being sidelined when chaos spikes.
- Goals: Keep the group safe and aligned; discern signal from noise in the madness.
- Relationships: Calms Sam; affirms Greg; steadying presence for Adam and Nick.
- Arc Seeds: Steps forward decisively when only intuition can parse the moment.

#### Nick Braswell
- Summary: Big, calm "teddy bear"; Seth Rogen-like laugh; rolls the best blunts; morale and protection.
- Personality/Voice: Laid-back humor; observational jokes; reluctant bruiser.
- Visual Cues: Larger silhouette; warm expressions; relaxed posture.
- Strengths: Physical presence; morale booster; resilient under pressure.
- Vulnerabilities: Avoids conflict until it’s unavoidable; doubts own instincts.
- Goals: Keep friends fed, laughing, and safe; don’t let the vibes break.
- Relationships: Comic foil/anchor for Adam; steady muscle for the team; duo dynamic akin to Jay & Silent Bob/Shaggy & his pal (vibe only, no explicit mention on-page).
- Arc Seeds: Turns reluctance into assertive protection when lines are crossed.

#### Adam Blade Capuana
- Summary: Overalls and bucket hat; artistic/creative observer; narrator voice; practical organizer.
- Personality/Voice: Wry, self-aware; keeps plans on track; narrates with festival-savvy humor.
- Visual Cues: Overalls/bucket hat motif; phone/maps/notes props.
- Strengths: Logistics; narration; converting insights into concrete steps.
- Vulnerabilities: Overplanning during chaos; uses jokes to dodge stress.
- Goals: Get the squad where they need to be—alive, together, and on time for Paul.
- Relationships: Operationalizes Greg; amplifies Nick; respects Dani; corrals Sam.
- Arc Seeds: Shifts from observer to decisive field lead when the storm hits.
