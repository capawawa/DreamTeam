## DreamTeam Writer’s Book — Master Structure

Purpose: A prescriptive, production-ready outline that tells writers exactly what to produce, how long it should be, and where to anchor it to sources.

Notes on citations: All source links follow the house convention. If a stable line anchor is unknown, links include “line TBD”.

### 1. Property Overview and Canon Grounding

- Purpose: Align every writer on premise, spine, and POV/canon rules before any pages are scripted.
- Inputs:
  - ["DreamTeam_Research_Inventory.md"](docs/research/DreamTeam_Research_Inventory.md:1-130)
  - ["Series_Treatment.md"](docs/treatment/Series_Treatment.md:1-16)
  - ["Canon_Rules.md"](docs/canon/Canon_Rules.md:1-22)
- Deliverables:
  - 1-page premise recap (bullet form).
  - 1-page “Canon One-Pager” summarizing perception tiers and ambiguity discipline.
- Page/length targets and checklists:
  - 2 pages total; checklist: premise, five-issue spine, perception tiers, ambiguity rule.
- Continuity hooks:
  - Update ambiguity policy references wherever corruption is depicted; add Matt POV counterpoints in planned beats.
- Approvals:
  - Gate: Premise/Canon sign-off. See ["Production_Plan.md"](production/Production_Plan.md:line TBD).

### 2. Team, Antagonists, and Encounters

- Purpose: Lock character functions, antagonistic vectors, and encounter taxonomy to drive repeatable beats.
- Inputs:
  - ["Character_Bible.md"](docs/characters/Character_Bible.md:line TBD)
  - ["ManInRed_Dossier.md"](docs/man-in-red/ManInRed_Dossier.md:line TBD)
  - ["Bands_and_Encounters.md"](docs/encounters/Bands_and_Encounters.md:line TBD)
  - ["Festival_Missions.md"](docs/missions/Festival_Missions.md:1-11)
  - ["ManInRed_Encounter.md"](docs/man-in-red/ManInRed_Encounter.md:line TBD)
- Deliverables:
  - 2-page character briefs per principal (Core Five, Nicole, Matt) with do/don’t lists.
  - 1-page Man in Red function sheet (catalyst rules).
  - 2-page Encounters Catalogue: biergarten perimeter, vendor exception, silent disco anomalies, strobe confrontations.
- Page/length targets and checklists:
  - Each principal: 2 pages; Man in Red: 1 page; Encounters: 2 pages.
  - Checklist: goals, tactics, limits, tells, sample beats, taboo boundaries.
- Continuity hooks:
  - Tie encounter outcomes to beat sheets; flag any new motifs for Canon updates.
- Approvals:
  - Gate: Cast/Antagonists lock. See ["Production_Plan.md"](production/Production_Plan.md:line TBD).

### 3. World, Setting, and Visual Language

- Purpose: Make location use and visual motifs consistent across scripts.
- Inputs:
  - ["Firedog_Setting.md"](docs/setting/Firedog_Setting.md:line TBD)
  - ["Perception_Details.md"](docs/perception/Perception_Details.md:line TBD)
  - ["Comic_Script_Format.md"](scripts/format/Comic_Script_Format.md:1-25)
- Deliverables:
  - 2-page map notes with staging layers (storm-risk, thicket gates, biergarten ingress, silent disco boundaries, food truck row, main-stage approach).
  - 1-page visual language primer (overlays, Matt clean lens, motif palette).
- Page/length targets and checklists:
  - 3 pages total; checklist: map layers, access paths, crowd flow, motif usage examples.
- Continuity hooks:
  - Align motif usage with ambiguity policy; add Matt POV counters within 1–2 pages of any “corruption” paneling.
- Approvals:
  - Gate: Setting/Visual lock. See ["Production_Plan.md"](production/Production_Plan.md:line TBD).

### 4. Theme, Tone, Rating and Taboo Boundaries

- Deliverables:
  - 1-page tone board (bullets) with comps.

- Page/length targets and checklists:
  - 2 pages total; checklist: horror ceiling, comedy floor, romance limits, drug depiction rules.
- Continuity hooks:
  - Flag any script notes that risk collapsing ambiguity or breaching rating.
- Approvals:
  - Gate: Tone/Rating lock. See ["Production_Plan.md"](production/Production_Plan.md:line TBD).
